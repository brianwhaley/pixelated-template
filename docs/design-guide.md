## 1. Brand Visual Identity

### The "Lowcountry Estate" Palette

To capture the South Carolina feel, we are moving away from "bright lawnmower green" (which signals basic mowing) toward deep, rich tones that signal established wealth, history, and hardscaping materials.

#### Primary Color: Live Oak Deep Green
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #1F3A26;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#1F3A26`
- **Usage**: Logos, heavy headers, footer backgrounds. Represents stability and year-round health.
</div>

#### Secondary Color: Spanish Moss
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #8A9A91;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#8A9A91`
- **Usage**: Sub-headers, icons, secondary buttons. A soft, gray-green that evokes the SC coastline.
</div>

#### Accent 1: Lowcountry Brick
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #A35C48;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#A35C48`
- **Usage**: Call-to-action buttons (CTAs), highlights. Represents the hardscaping/masonry focus (red clay and historic brick).
</div>

#### Accent 2: Marsh Grass Gold
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #C5B386;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#C5B386`
- **Usage**: Thin lines, borders, premium badges. Adds the "High-End" luxury feel without being shiny gold.
</div>

#### Background: Oyster Shell White
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #F4F3EF; border: 2px solid #ddd;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#F4F3EF`
- **Usage**: Main page backgrounds. Not a sterile clinical white; a warm, natural cream.
</div>

#### Text Color: Wrought Iron
<div class="color-swatch" style="background-color: #2C2C2C;"></div>
<div class="color-info">
- **Hex**: `#2C2C2C`
- **Usage**: All body text. Softer than pure black for better readability.
</div>

### Typography

The font pairing mixes tradition (SC history) with modern efficiency (Commercial reliability).

#### Headings: Playfair Display
A high-contrast serif font. It feels editorial, expensive, and classic.

<div class="font-sample playfair">

<h2 style="margin: 5px 0; font-size: 1.5em;">Structural Integrity</h2>

A clean, geometric sans-serif. It is easy to read on mobile and looks professional and corporate.

### Social Media Strategy
- **Content Focus**: Before/after project photos, hardscaping installations, tree care demonstrations, and local SC community events
- **Posting Frequency**: 2-3 posts per week across platforms
- **Target Audience**: Commercial property managers, HOA boards, and high-end residential clients in Charleston and surrounding areas
- **Brand Voice**: Professional, reliable, and knowledgeable - emphasizing expertise in commercial landscaping and hardscaping
